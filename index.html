Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Cavs below. By clicking on a Cav you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Cav's points is hidden from you until you click on it.

When the game starts, the game will change the values of the Cavs points.
